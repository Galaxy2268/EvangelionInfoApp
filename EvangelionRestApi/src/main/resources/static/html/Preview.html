Evangelion Info REST API available endpoints:

Characters Endpoint:

  • /api/characters - (gives you a list of available characters)
  • /api/characters/name/{name} - (gives you a specific character by name)
  • /api/characters/id/{id} - (gives you a specific character by id)

Evangelions Endpoint:

  • /api/evangelions - (gives you a list of evangelions)
  • /api/evangelions/name/{name} - (gives you a specific evangelion by name)
  • /api/evangelions/id/{id} - (gives you a specific evangelion by id)

Episodes Endpoint:

  • /api/episodes - (gives you a list of episodes)
  • /api/episodes/number/{number} - (gives you a specific episode by number)
  • /api/episodes/id/{id} - (gives you a specific episode by id)

Angels Endpoint:

  • /api/angels - (gives you a list of angels)
  • /api/angels/name/{name} - (gives you a specific angel by name)
  • /api/angels/id/{id} - (gives you a specific angel by id)

Stuff Endpoint:

  • /api/stuff - (gives you a list of stuff)
  • /api/stuff/name/{name} - (gives you a specific stuff by name)
  • /api/stuff/id/{id} - (gives you a specific stuff by id)
